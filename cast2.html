Tokyo

  1. Name: Tokyo
  2. Details:

    Silene Oliveira, better known by her code-name Tokyo ( es. Tokio ), is one of the main protagonists of the Netflix series Money Heist, as well as the narrator of the story. She is a member of the heist crew set to rob the Royal Mint of Spain.

  3. Biography:

    A the age of 14, Tokyo joined her 28 year old boyfriend into a world of crime. Together, they successfully pulled off 15 heists.
