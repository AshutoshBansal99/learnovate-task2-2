Rio

  • Name: Rio
  • Details:

    Aníbal Cortés, also known by his alias, Rio ( es. Río ), is one of the main characters in the Netflix series Money Heist, portrayed by actor Miguel Herrán.

  • Biography:

    Rio was born in 1997 in Getafe. His nickname given by his mother was Rayo, meaning lightning bolt in Spanish. At young age he became interested in computers and began programming at the age of 6.[1] He often locked himself in his room for hours. His parents believed that he was working or playing on his computer, but he was actually beginning his career as a hacker.
