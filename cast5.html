Denver

  1. Name: Denver
  2. Details:

    Daniel Ramos, better known by his alias, Denver, is one of the main characters in the Netflix series Money Heist, portrayed by actor Jaime Lorente.

  3. Biography:

    Denver was raised by his father Moscow, who abandoned Denver's mother at a roundabout due her drug use. For many years, Moscow led Denver to believe that his mother abandoned him.
